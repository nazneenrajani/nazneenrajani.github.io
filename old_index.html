About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Texas, Austin. I am interested in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. My advisor is Ray Mooney and I work on Slot Filling (SF) as part of Knowledge Base Population (KBP) using Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. I completed my MS thesis with Prof. Jason Baldridge on extracting topics from Twitter based on author and recipient of tweets. In the past I have also worked with Prof. Inderjit Dhillon on analysis of Social Networks. I am a recipient of the prestigious Google Anita Borg Scholarship. Here is my resume.
